Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your personal information.

Information We Collect

Our app collects and uses your identity information solely to personalize your user experience within the app. This includes:

  • User profile information (name, preferences)
  • App usage data for personalization purposes

OneDrive Access

Our app accesses your OneDrive to provide its core functionality. It is important to note that:

  • Data accessed from your OneDrive is not sent to any external servers or third parties
  • Your OneDrive data is used exclusively for the purpose of this app's functionality
  • We do not store, transmit, or use your OneDrive data for any other purposes
  • Your OneDrive files and information remain under your complete control

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to enhance your experience with our app through personalization. We do not:

  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Send your data to external servers
  • Track your activity for advertising purposes
  • Use your data to provide any external services

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Your information never leaves your device and remains under your complete control.

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not provide, sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your information is kept strictly confidential and is used solely within the app for personalization purposes.

Tracking and Analytics

Our app does not track your activities, collect analytics data, or use any tracking technologies. Your privacy is fully protected, and your app usage remains private.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers, the security of your data is primarily determined by the security measures on your device. We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate passwords and security settings.

Your Rights

As all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information. You can:

  • Delete the app at any time to remove all associated data
  • Modify your profile information within the app
  • Reset app settings to clear personalization data

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or store personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Support page for contact information.
